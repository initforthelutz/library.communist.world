    TO N. I. BUKHARIN


    Comrade Bukharin,
        I send you today's Pravda. Now, why print stupidities in the guise of the article by Pletnyov,[427] who puts on pompous airs with all the learned and fashionable words he can? I have marked two stupidities and put a number of question marks. The author has to learn not "proletarian" science, but simply to learn. Can it be that the editorial board of Pravda is not going to explain the author's mistakes to him? Why, this is falsification of historical materialism! Playing at historical materialism!

    Yours,        
    Lenin
